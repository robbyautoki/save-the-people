### About
At Save the People Worldwide, we believe that every human being has the power to make a difference.

You don't need wealth, status, or special skills to help — every gesture, every contribution, every act of kindness matters.

Founded by Katharina Erdem, this charity was created with one mission: to bring hope, support, and dignity to children and families in need across the globe.

Every day, millions struggle with hunger, lack of clean water, unsafe living conditions, and limited access to education and healthcare. We work to change that — directly, transparently, and with compassion.

### What We Do
- Provide essential food and daily meals for children
- Support hospitals, clinics, and emergency care
- Create safe spaces and education programs for kids
- Bring clean water, hygiene support, and basic medical supplies to underserved communities
- Offer fast-response aid in crisis situations
